Guacamole

Fri 29 July 2022 #Food #Recipe

Makes 8 servings.

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt

Add 1 avocado to a bowl and mash it with a fork. Add lime juice and salt to bowl and mix together. Chop remaining avocados into big chunks. Add them to the bowl and mix everything together. Serve.
