Sidecar

Tue 19 July 2022 #food #recipe

Makes 1 serving.

  • 2 shots Grand Marnier
  • 1 shot lemon juice
  • 1 ice cube
  • sugar

Sugared Glass

Rub lemon along rim of cocktail glass. Dip rim of cocktail glass in plate of sugar.

Drink

Add ice cube to Yeti Rambler. Squeeze a lemon and pour through strainer into a shot glass to measure. Add lemon juice to rambler. Add Grand Marnier to rambler. Close lid. Shake for 30 seconds. Pour into sugared glass. Serve.
