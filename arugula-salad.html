Arugula Salad

Thu 14 July 2022 #Food #Recipe

Makes 2 servings.

  • 5 ounces baby arugula
  • 1 cup cherry tomotoes
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon Terrapin Ridge Farms ginger miso dressing

Add arugula to a large salad bowl. Remove pit from avocado and scoop a tablespoon at a time into bowl. Cut tomotoes in half and add to bowl. Drizzle dressing over salad. Toss the salad. Serve immediately.
