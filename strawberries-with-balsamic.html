Strawberries With Balsamic

Thu 28 July 2022 #food #recipe

Makes 8 servings. Eat it on its own or over a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

  • 4 cups fresh strawberries
  • 4 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 0.25 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Mix sugar, vinegar, and pepper in a large bowl. Wash strawberries, cut off stems, and slice into thick pieces. Toss strawberries with vinegar mixture. Serve.
